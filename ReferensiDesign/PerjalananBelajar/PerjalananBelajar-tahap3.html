Microplastics: The Invisible Crisis

As we enter the deeper ocean currents, our sensors detect particles smaller than 1 micrometer. These fragments are no longer just floating debris; they have become part of the water's fluid state. Current technology cannot physically "catch" or filter them without destroying the microorganisms that form the base of the food chain.
